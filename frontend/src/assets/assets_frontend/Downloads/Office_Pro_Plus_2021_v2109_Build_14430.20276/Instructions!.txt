HOW TO INSTALL ?

1. Download the MS Office with activator.
2. Extract the archive to get setup files.
2. Install MS Office from given setup.
3. Close the office installer and office after installation (if running)
4. Extract the activator zip file and run the given activator as admin.
5. Convert the MS Office to VL.
6. Use the activator to activate your MS Office.
7. Wait for sometime.. it'll do its magic to activate office.
8. Boom! Product Activated.. Enjoy now.


Note - The Office files are tested with Malwarebytes
